Why Would SData be Turned Off?

Some Saleslogix entities have their SData feed turned on by default. You can turn the SData feed for an entity on or off on the SData tab when you edit the entity.

Some Saleslogix entities cannot be added to the SData feed (and thus do not  have an SData tab) because they have a composite key. The Sage Data (SData) specification requires a single unique key due to the difficulty in composing a URI with multiple keys. If you need SData for an entity that has a composite key, consider adding a default primary key to the entity schema.
