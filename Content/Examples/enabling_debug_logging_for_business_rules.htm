Enabling Debug Logging for Business Rules

To debug business rules in Visual Studio, you can enable logging in the log4net.config file.

To enable

  1. In a text editor, open the log4net.config file of the portal. (For example, C:\Inetpub\wwwroot\SlxClient\log4net.config)
  2. Remove commenting from the <appender>, <root>, and <logger> sections.
  3. Add the following <logger> section and save your changes:

<logger name="BusinessRules">

                <level value="DEBUG" />

                <appender-ref ref="LOG" />

</logger>

 

Logging statements will be written to debug.log under the "log" folder of the website. (For example, C:\Inetpub\wwwroot\SLXClient\log\debug.log)
